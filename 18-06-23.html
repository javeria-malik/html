    • Artificial intelligence

    Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems or machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include problem-solving, learning, reasoning, perception, understanding natural language, and decision-making.





    cloud computing

    Cloud computing refers to the delivery of computing services over the internet. Instead of relying on local servers or personal computers, cloud computing allows users to access and use computing resources, such as storage, processing power, and software applications, from remote servers hosted by cloud service providers.





    metaverse

    The term "metaverse" refers to a virtual reality space where users can interact with a computer-generated environment and other users in real-time. It is often described as a collective virtual shared space that encompasses the virtual worlds, augmented reality, and the internet.
